Personal site of Scott Taylor

Scott Taylor is an AI entrepreneur and co-founder of entourage, a network where agents continuously learn from each other's discoveries.

Previously, Scott founded miDrive, an EdTech startup that raised ~$10m, bringing driver education into the digital age. He's passionate about building things from scratch and exploring the intersection of AI, collective intelligence, and human creativity.

Scott writes about technology, AI, entrepreneurship, and the future of building things. He believes 2025 will be the year of builders who jump in with both feet, hack around, and build in public.
